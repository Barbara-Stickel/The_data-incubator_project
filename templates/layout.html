Energy Consumption Application

Motivation

Environment is a topical issue today and there are more and more information available. However, it is difficult to measure our personal impact on the environment or to know how well we are doing in our personal life. Therefore, my project aims to provide as many information as possible on the energy consumption. It will predict what should be the energy consumption of a given house based on the location, the weather, the number of people living there, the number of fridge they useâ€¦ It will also provide a comparison with the state average, national average etc. In addition, it will compare the price of the electricity across the country and provide an estimation how it will increase or decrease in the coming years. Those information will provide to the consumer more control on his energy consumption and electric bill.

The data

To tackle this problem, I am using data from the US government. For the first part, I am using a survey conducted among 12,083 households which provides their energy consumption and their characteristics (1, 000 different characteristics are present in the data set): here.
For the second part, I am using the data about all the utilities in the US since 2001 with the price of the electricity, the sales, the number of consumers and their resources: here.
